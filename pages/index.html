Welcome to my Portfolio

Hi, my name's Jo. If you're interested in poetry, especially French poets, then have a look round my site.

This is a fan girl site for Andree Chedid, a poet I knew nothing about a month ago. I'm going to use this portfolio project to collect some of her poems, thoughts and anything else I'm loving right now.

Oh and yes, this site is also a dedication to Pantone colour of the year and I'm going to have some fun with colour but I'll try not to go overboard.

Andree Chedid famously said that her work was a quest for humanity. I can't claim anything so noble but maybe this project will help me leave the matrix every once in a while.

Qui reste debout ?

1968

                D'abord,
                Efface ton nom
                Abolis ton âge
                Supprime tes lieux
                Déracine ce que tu sembles
                Qui reste debout ?
                Maintenant,
                Ressaisis ton nom
                Revêts ton âge
                Adopte ta maison
                Pénètre ta marche
                Et puis…
                À n'en plus finir,
                Recommence ?

Who is Andree Chedid?

A month ago I knew nothing about this French poet. I was searching for French female poets and her name came up. She sounded cool so I thought I'd find out more. I was also interested by her Syrian Lebanese Egyptian background.

Works

Andree Chedid was profilic, she wrote poetry, novels and theatre plays. She was a celebrated poet in her lifetime in France and very popular with children. Many French primary schools carry her name.
